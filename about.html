Our Story

LeetMath was born from a simple observation: while programming interview prep resources are abundant, there's a gap in practice platforms for electrical engineering and mathematics students. Founded in 2023 by a group of electrical engineering graduates, LeetMath aims to bridge this gap by providing a comprehensive platform for students and professionals to practice, learn, and master engineering concepts through problem-solving.

Our platform offers a structured approach to learning complex mathematical and electrical engineering concepts through practical problem-solving. By focusing on industry-relevant problems and providing interactive solutions, we help students develop the critical thinking and analytical skills needed in today's competitive technical landscape.

Our Mission

To create an accessible, comprehensive platform that empowers electrical engineering and mathematics students to hone their problem-solving skills through practice, collaboration, and expert guidance.

Our Vision

A world where every engineering student has access to quality practice resources, enabling them to build confidence, excel in their academics, and become innovative problem solvers in their careers.
